Lash Extension Aftercare

Follow these simple steps to keep your lashes looking beautiful and maximize retention:

  • Wait 24 hours: Avoid water, steam, and excessive heat immediately after application to allow adhesive to fully cure.
  • Cleanse Daily: Gently wash lashes with a lash-safe cleanser and water. Clean lashes last longer!
  • Brush Daily: Use a clean spoolie brush to gently groom lashes, keeping them fluffy and separated.
  • Schedule Fills: Book refills every 2-3 weeks to maintain fullness and replace naturally shed lashes.
  • Avoid oil-based products (makeup, cleansers) near the eyes. Oil breaks down the adhesive.
  • Avoid waterproof mascara and liquid/gel eyeliners on extensions.
  • Avoid mechanical lash curlers.
  • Avoid picking, pulling, or rubbing your eyes/lashes.
